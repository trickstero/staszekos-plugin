## Variance / BZA Precedent Protocol

1. Search `[city] board of zoning appeals decisions` + year range
2. Search `[city] BZA [district] [use type] variance granted`
3. Look for: same district, similar hardship, same use category
4. Pull: case number, date, address, relief granted, hardship finding cited
5. Format in memo Section 20 as: "Case No. [X], [date], [address] — [relief type] granted on finding of [hardship language]"

## Historic Tax Credit Research Workflow

### Federal 20% HTC (NPS)
1. Confirm NR listing: nps.gov/subjects/nationalregister
2. Confirm contributing status if historic district
3. Review Part 1 / Part 2 / Part 3 application requirements at nps.gov/tps/tax-incentives

### State HTC
1. Search `[state] historic tax credit program [year]`
2. Check: percentage, cap, transferability, pass-through entities, sunset date
3. Note: IL = 25% state credit (active). DC = 15–20%. Check others at time of project.
